Production Studio: KNOCK-OUT ARCADE

KNOCK-OUT ARCADE is a pixel-art fighting game that aims to bring new players in with simplified controls and engaging single player content. The project was headed by Ryland Kelly and was present for 2 semesters of production studio.
For both of those semesters, I worked on the project as a design lead. I help create design documents for the game's mechanics and playable characters as well as assisting other designers in any way i could. More importatnly though, I created place holder animations for 3 of the playable characters give programmers something to work with while the artists were busy creating polished sprites. You can view these placeholder animations by pressing the button below.



While I'm no longer apart of the project anymore, Ryland Kelly is still continuing its development. The game currently has a demo on Itch.io and you can also view dev logs for the game on Ryland's youtube channel
